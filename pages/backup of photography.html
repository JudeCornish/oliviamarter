Acrylic Painting

Acrylic painting has been my medium of choice since GCSEs and A Level study. I particularly enjoy working with a palette knife as I feel the texture of it adds dimension to the work. Within my acrylic painting I have explored themes of cultures, dementia, portraits, landscapes and nature.
